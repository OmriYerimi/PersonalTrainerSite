Deltoids

Here are some of the best exercises for the deltoids, and how I recommend doing them:

  1. Side Dummbell lateral raise:

    • Stand straight with the dumbbells against your body.
    • Lift your arms slowly until you cross your shoulders (ears high).
    • Stay there for 2 sec.
    • Then slowly and controlled lower down your arms.

  2. Side Cable lateral raise

    • Place the cable at pelvic height.
    • Stand straight with one cable against your body.
    • Lift your arm slowly until you cross your shoulder.
    • Stay there for 2 sec.
    • Then slowly and controlled lower down your arm.
