Pectoralise Major

Here are some of the best exercises for the pecs, and how I recommend doing them:

  1. Incline Dummbell Bench Press:

    • Place the sit at a 30-degree angle.
    • Take the dumbbells and place them upright on your upper chest (just under your chin).
    • Make sure your shoulder blades are tucked into the chair (like they are trying to catch your towel).
    • Now slowly let the weights down until you feel a good stretch on your pecs and shoulders.
    • Count for 3 sec and only then push the weights up against your upper chest.

  2. Dummbell Bench Press

    • Place the sit at a 180-degree angle.
    • Take the dumbbells and place them upright on your middle chest.
    • Make sure your shoulder blades are tucked into the chair (like they are trying to catch your towel).
    • Now slowly let the weights down until you feel a good stretch on your pecs and shoulders.
    • Count for 3 sec and only then push the weights up against your middle chest.
